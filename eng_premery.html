les contes et
les·gens·de premery


Partnership: EBE 58 and Premery town hall

 With: Antoine Behaghel, Jean-Louis Pouilles

"Les contes et les·gens·de Premery" is a forward-thinking project, a cultural program designed for a town in the Nièvre region and its inhabitants. It includes permanent installations and an annual festival that document and tell the stories of the region. 20 narratives linking local myths and contemporary anecdotes will place the inhabitants of the village at the center of their history. We propose focusing on a more lively and personal history to reconnect humans with their territory.
